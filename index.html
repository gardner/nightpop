Night Pop

The Quiet Magic of Winter Night

Welcome to Night Pop, a concept that reimagines how we spend our winter evenings. This is not your typical night market. There are no vendors, no transactions, and no alcohol. Instead, we offer an alternative space that invites you to bask in the quiet, warm glow of community and calm.


What is Night Pop?

Every Friday from 7 to 9 pm, we transform a small corner of Nelson into a cozy, inviting sanctuary. Think of it as a brief escape from the hustle and bustle, a place where warm lights illuminate the night, and soft music fills the air. It's a free space, meticulously crafted to be clean and serene, where the focus is on connection and tranquility.


Night Pop 2024 - Nelson

Every Friday night, somewhere in Nelson, you’ll find Night Pop. Follow the glow of warm lights and the sound of gentle music to a space that’s designed to be an oasis of calm. Remember, we don’t sell anything. We’re here to create an experience, and we leave each location better than we found it.

Join Us

  • Bring Your Own Cup: In the spirit of sustainability, we offer free hot chocolate, but there’s a catch—bring your own cup. It’s a small step towards reducing waste and a gentle reminder of the value in the little things.
  • Live Music, Unplugged: Our soundscape is created by local musicians with unamplified instruments. The result is a soothing, organic sound that perfectly complements the calm atmosphere.
  • Stay Informed: Night Pop is a nomadic experience. Each week, we pop up in a different location. Follow us on Instagram for updates and discover where we'll be next.
  • Bring Your Own Seat: We have a few chairs, but we encourage you to bring your own for added comfort. This is your space to relax and unwind.
